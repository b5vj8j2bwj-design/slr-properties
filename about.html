SLR Properties

SLR Properties is a renovation-focused real estate company based in the Coachella Valley, with a deep appreciation for mid-century homes throughout Palm Springs and the surrounding desert communities.

I’m drawn to properties with strong architectural identity — clean lines, thoughtful layouts, and a real sense of place. Many of the homes I work on have incredible bones but need careful restoration and modern refinement.

Each renovation is approached with intention, patience, and attention to detail. The goal is always to honor the original architecture while adapting the home for modern living.
